The school was started by Smt Leelavathi in the year 1969. The school is located in Malleshwaram, Banglore and has 3 buildings of its own for teaching. The 1st building teaches classes from pre nursery to 4th. The 2nd building teaches classes from 5th to 7th. And the 3rd building teaches classes from 8th to 10th.
